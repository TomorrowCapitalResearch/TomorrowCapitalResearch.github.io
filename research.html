Tomorrow Capital Research

At Tomorrow Capital Research, we treat the firm as a single integrated system rather than a set of isolated desks. Our research is organised into four connected layers: Business Strategy Portfolio Management, Trading Strategies and Execution.

Each layer has a clear job, but they constantly feed information to one another so the entire system can adapt as markets and competitors change.

A Better Deal for Retail Equities

For retail equity investors, market makers regularly offer a better price on securities than the public exchanges. This is known as price improvement.

The benefits of price improvement flow directly into investors’ pockets. Our work helps reduce the cost of market participation and increase access to financial opportunity.

Our clients seek the best execution. Our efforts to deliver ultimately benefit the entire marketplace.

The Business of Tomorrow Capital Research

Business & Expansion Strategy

We sequence our expansion across asset classes and business lines, treating each new venture as an option that must earn its place in the portfolio of businesses.

Data, simulations and risk constraints guide when and how we grow, not fashion or hype.

Portfolio Management System

Our portfolio engine allocates capital across strategies, assets and time horizons, optimising for return, risk, liquidity and diversification at the same time.

It continuously rebalances as regimes and market conditions change.

Trading Strategies

We maintain a library of systematic strategies across styles: market-making, statistical arbitrage, trend, mean-reversion and event-driven models.

A structured research pipeline moves ideas from backtest to controlled live deployment.

Execution & Microstructure

Our execution layer handles smart routing, order scheduling and safety checks so that strategies interact with markets efficiently and securely.

Detailed fill and impact metrics feed back into strategy and portfolio decisions.

One System, Four Layers

From top to bottom, Business Strategy sets direction, Portfolio Management allocates capital, Trading Strategies generate orders, and Execution connects to markets.

From bottom to top, detailed trading and execution data flows back into the system, allowing each layer to learn, adapt and refine its decisions. The research objective is to make this loop faster, more stable and more intelligent over time.
