Careers at Tomorrow Capital

Trading & Research Roles

We’re building a focused team to explore short- and medium-horizon opportunities across crypto, FX, and equities. You’ll work inside our adaptive portfolio and execution frameworks, with the freedom to follow the data.

Algorithmic Trader — London

Design, test, and run live trading strategies across global markets. You’ll work within our adaptive execution and portfolio frameworks to explore new alpha ideas and market microstructures.

  • Strong quantitative reasoning and comfort with uncertainty.
  • Fluency in Python, R, or a similar analytical language.
  • Passion for markets and data-driven strategy design.

What matters most: curiosity, discipline, and the will to learn faster than markets evolve.

Quantitative Researcher — HFT / MFT (Crypto · FX · Equities) — London

Research short- and medium-frequency trading signals using advanced statistical and machine-learning methods. You will prototype and test new strategies within our high-performance simulation environment.

  • Strong analytical and mathematical foundation.
  • Experience with Python, C++, or similar quantitative tools.
  • Understanding of markets or data-science concepts.

What matters most: independent thinking, scientific rigour, and the drive to explore new frontiers.

Engineering & Operations Roles

Under the surface, Tomorrow Capital runs on robust systems and precise operations. These roles turn research ideas into reliable infrastructure and keep the firm running smoothly.

Quantitative Developer (C++) — London

Engineer low-latency systems and scalable research tools powering our multi-asset trading engine. Collaborate with researchers to turn models into production-grade pipelines.

  • Proficiency in modern C++ (17/20) and multithreaded design.
  • Knowledge of data structures, performance tuning, and networking.
  • Interest in quantitative finance and real-time systems.

What matters most: clean code, curiosity, and ownership from concept to deployment.

HR / Payroll / Accounting — London

Join the core operations team supporting our growing research and trading organization. You’ll manage payroll, accounting records, and employee logistics with precision and trust.

  • Experience or interest in HR, finance, or bookkeeping systems.

  • Comfort with Excel / Sheets and modern HR software.
