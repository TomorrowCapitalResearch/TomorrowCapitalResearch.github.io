Research, architecture, execution

Our stack spans signal research (stat‑arb, trend, market microstructure), portfolio construction (risk parity, Kelly‑aware tilts, CVaR limits), and execution (adaptive market making, slippage‑aware scheduling). We combine interpretable models with ML where it adds edge, under strict controls and monitoring.

  • Data — clean pipelines, point‑in‑time features, robust backtests.
  • Signals — cross‑sectional & time‑series alphas, regime & risk models.
  • Execution — smart order routing, liquidity & inventory control.
  • Governance — change control, notebooks‑to‑prod, real‑time telemetry.
