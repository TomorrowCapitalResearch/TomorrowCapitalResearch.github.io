Who We Are

We’re a small team of traders, researchers and technologists building the next generation of algorithmic finance. We design, test and run systems that tackle some of the most interesting problems in modern markets.

OUR PRINCIPLES

Aligned, Ethical Trading

We build strategies that respect market structure and counterparties. We avoid hidden risks, opaque products, and behaviour that misaligns us from our partners or clients. Integrity is a constraint, not a trade-off.

Scientific Rigor

Decisions are driven by data, not ego. We design experiments, measure uncertainty, and change our minds when the evidence demands it. Clear thinking and honest backtests matter more than clever stories.

Curiosity Without Boundaries

Great ideas rarely fit neatly inside a job title. People at Tomorrow Capital explore markets, math, engineering, and design wherever their curiosity leads—then share what they learn with the team.

Low Ego, High Ownership

We keep hierarchy light and collaboration strong. Everyone is expected to take responsibility for their work, admit mistakes quickly, and help others succeed. We win as a team or not at all.
