We are a focused research and trading firm. To protect deep work and our team’s time, this contact page is deliberately narrow in scope.

When not to contact us

  • Recruitment & headhunters: We are not currently hiring and we do not work with external recruiters. For future hiring needs, we have chosen to partner with Alexander Chapman.
  • Investors: We are not raising capital and do not respond to unsolicited investment approaches. If we believe there is a mutual fit, we will reach out first.
  • Sales & vendors: We prefer to discover data providers, infrastructure partners and tooling through our own research and in-person conversations. Please do not use this form for cold sales outreach.

Who we’d love to hear from

If any of the following describe you, we’d be very happy to hear from you:

  • Researchers, quants or engineers with ideas that intersect our conjectures.
  • Partners for genuine, long-term collaborations rather than short-term transactions.
  • Creatives and designers interested in the intersection of markets, data and storytelling.
  • Aspiring quants or curious individuals who want to exchange thoughts and build a network.

If that sounds like you, send us a short note explaining who you are, what you’re working on, and why you think we should speak.
