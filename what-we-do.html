What We Do

We design and operate algorithmic trading systems across digital assets, FX, equities and alternative markets — uniting quantitative research, AI and engineering to turn complexity into opportunity.

Algorithmic trading, end to end

Tomorrow Capital runs a fully integrated research–to–execution pipeline. We build, test and deploy systematic strategies across multiple time horizons:

  • Short-horizon HFT / MFT strategies and market-making.
  • Medium-horizon statistical and machine-learning strategies.
  • Longer-horizon systematic portfolios and cross-asset themes.

Where we focus

Our systems monitor and trade:

  • Crypto and digital assets
  • FX and precious metals
  • Equities and ETFs
  • Selective alternative & sports markets

Every strategy is built with explicit objectives: risk-adjusted return, robustness, and adaptability to changing regimes.

The Tomorrow framework: four decision layers

Markets are complex, so our architecture is layered. We optimise the business, the portfolio, the strategies and the execution — each with its own models, yet fully connected.

Layer I

Expansion & Ventures

Decide where to play: new markets, products and business lines. We quantify opportunity size, competitive dynamics and risk before committing capital or engineering time.

Layer II

Portfolio & Capital Allocation

Allocate capital across asset classes, strategies and horizons. We use portfolio optimisation and scenario analysis to balance growth, drawdown and liquidity.

Layer III

Strategy & Signal Research

Design and validate trading signals using statistical learning, microstructure analytics and physics-inspired models. Robustness beats anecdotes; empirical results drive decisions.

Layer IV

Execution & Microstructure

Turn signals into fills. We optimise order placement, routing and inventory using real-time market data, with strict controls on slippage, impact and latency.

Research, AI & infrastructure

Tomorrow Capital is a research and engineering company as much as it is a trading firm. Our edge comes from the way we model markets, the tools we build, and the data we maintain.

Quantitative research

We combine classical statistics with modern machine learning: regime detection, market-state models, point processes, reinforcement learning and multi-agent simulations.

Technology & data

From low-latency C++ components to Python research stacks, our infrastructure is built to iterate quickly and deploy safely. We maintain structured, high-resolution data across our markets.

Risk & governance

Risk is treated as a first-class object: pre-trade constraints, live monitoring and post-trade analysis are embedded at every layer, from individual strategy to firm-wide capital.

Who we work with

Over time, we aim to partner with allocators, institutions and founders who share our belief that finance can be rebuilt on systematic, transparent and intelligent foundations.
